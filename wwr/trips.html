Find Your Next Adventure

Rapids Avenue Come take a raft down the river with intermittent rapids (no higher than Class 2) and slower sections with beautiful views. This adventure is beginner friendly and takes approximately 3 hours to complete. Must be at least 8 years old to participate.
The Rush For the more experienced rafter, this adventure takes you through a series of Class 3 and Class 4 rapids. Prepare to get wet! This adventure takes approximately 2 hours and participants must be at least 16 years old.
The Splash Zone For our most experienced rafters, this adventure takes you through several intense series of rapids, interspersed with periods of calm with beautiful views. Prepare to get wet! This adventure takes approximately 6 hours and participants must be at least 16 years old to participate.
Family Day Trip Our most family friendly adventure, this adventure takes you through a long slow stretch of river where you can relax, enjoy, the sunshine, and enjoy the occasional small rapid. This adventure takes approximately 4 hours and paricipants must be at least 5 years old to participate.
The Grotto One of our most popular adventures, this adventure is a day-long trip with a side trip up a smaller branch of the river to explore the grotto. This fun adventure allows you to spend all day on the river. Participants must be at least 8 years old to participate.
